Method
Linear trend + volatility band
Simple and transparent on purpose — no hidden model.

Bearing fits a straight-line trend to your recent numbers and bands it by your own historical volatility. It's a fast read, not a guarantee — unusual events (holidays, promotions, weather, one-off news) won't be anticipated. Sanity-check big decisions against your own judgment.
